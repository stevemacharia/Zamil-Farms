Our Story

At Zamil Farms, we are committed to providing goat farmers with the tools and resources they need to succeed. By supplying fast-maturing and resilient goat breeds, we aim to help farmers increase their productivity and reduce the costs of raising goats. Our farm practices are held to the highest national standards for livestock production, and we are continuously working towards conforming with global standards as well. By supporting the success of goat farmers, we hope to improve the overall goat meat market and enhance the livelihoods of rural farmers in the process. Our dedication to excellence and sustainability in goat farming makes us the go-to source for top-quality breeds and expert guidance.

Our mission

Increase productivity and profitability of goat farming in Kenya.

Our vision

To promote production of good quality goats for rearing and slaughter.
