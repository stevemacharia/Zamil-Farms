About us

At Zamil Farms, we are committed to providing goat farmers with the tools and resources they need to succeed. By supplying fast-maturing and resilient goat breeds, we aim to help farmers increase their productivity and reduce the costs of raising goats. Our dedication to excellence and sustainability in goat farming makes us the go-to source for top-quality breeds and expert guidance.

Our mission

Increase productivity and profitability of goat farming in Kenya.

Our vision

To promote production of good quality goats for rearing and slaughter.
